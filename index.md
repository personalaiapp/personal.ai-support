# PersonalAI Support

Welcome to the official support page for **PersonalAI**.

PersonalAI is an **on-device AI application** designed for reading structured content in a clear and intuitive way.  
All AI processing runs **entirely on your device**, ensuring privacy and offline usability.

---

## About the App

PersonalAI is an on-device ML model reading app built to present AI-generated content in a structured and visually calm reading experience.

The app focuses on:

- On-device AI inference
- Privacy-first design
- Better formatted reading
- Intuitive user experience
- Depth-based content display
- Model switching
- Offline usage

PersonalAI runs **quantized AI models (GGUF format)** directly on your device.

No internet connection is required for AI processing.

## Troubleshooting

If you experience issues while using PersonalAI, try the following steps.

### Restart the Application
If the AI model stops responding or behaves unexpectedly, restarting the app usually resolves the issue.

### Check the Selected Model
1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Models**
3. Make sure a model is selected correctly.

### Slow Text Generation
If responses are slow:

- Select a **smaller model with fewer parameters**
- Smaller models run faster on mobile devices.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the app require an internet connection?
No. PersonalAI runs completely **offline** after the model is downloaded.

No. All AI processing happens **locally on your device**.

### Does the app collect personal data?
No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

### What models does the app support?
PersonalAI supports **quantized GGUF models** that can run efficiently on mobile devices.
